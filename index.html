Karachi Bachao Tehreek

Karachi Bachao Tehreek is a campaign to end the demolition of Karachi's working class settlements and markets.

Affected Area

Gujjar Nala

Gujjar Nala residents are facing forceful evictions, unlawful arrests, police brutality, and climate and infrastructural injustice. The current eviction campaign will render tens of thousands homeless.
